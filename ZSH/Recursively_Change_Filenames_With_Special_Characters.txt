====== Recursively Change Filenames With Special Characters ======

Content-Type: text/x-zim-wiki Wiki-Format: zim 0.4



# Recursively change filenames with characters ? [ ] / = + &lt; &gt; ; : " , - *



# autoload zmv autoload zmv $ chars='[][?=+&lt;&gt;;",-]' $ zmv '(**/)()' '$1${2//$~chars/%}'
